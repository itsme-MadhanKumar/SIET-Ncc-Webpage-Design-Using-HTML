The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a youth development movement in India that promotes discipline, leadership, and patriotism among students. Established under the NCC Act of 1948, it is a voluntary organization that trains students in military and community service activities. The NCC aims to instill values of unity, service, and responsibility, preparing cadets for various career paths, including the armed forces. Cadets participate in drills, camps, adventure activities, and social service programs, fostering physical fitness and a spirit of camaraderie. The motto "Unity and Discipline" encapsulates its core mission.
